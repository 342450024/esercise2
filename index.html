Why Shake

We love travelling & hate foreign transaction fees

Shake cards can be used in any of 80 currencies, online or in person, and you'll never pay foreign transaction fees. Ever. Cards can be used worldwide, where VISA cards are accepted (35+ million merchants).

We want to pay with our phones

Shake cards can be used anywhere tap & pay payments are accepted. All you need is an NFC-enabled smartphone and the world is yours to tap-tap-tap away.

We don't trust anyone with our credit cards

Bzzzt.. Bzzzt.. You are instantly notified for every accepted and declined transaction on your cards. Next time you worry your card got frauded, spin out a replacement card.

Shake is built for

The fiscally responsible

Set a limit on your cards like giving an allowance to your children. $20 on this card, €30 on that card, and you'll never get charged more.

Want to dispute a charge? One-click chargebacks are now a thing.

The world traveller

Travelling to Europe? Issue a card in Euros. After Europe you're going to Australia? Issue a new one in Australian Dollars. No longer are you penalized for being an outsider.

We support more than 20 currencies so you'll never feel stuck.

The security freakslike us

Time to laugh when a store gets a security breach. If they have your card saved, you can now fight back! Issue a new card, transfer your funds over, and voila! Your old card will disappear into the ether, balanceless.

Think of Shake like protection for your wallet.
